Our Vision

The Story

Valentine Owuor watched his mother — a retail trader in Nairobi — work harder than anyone he knew. She counted coins at the end of long days, calculating profit and loss in her head. She was just called a "small trader."

She wasn't small. She was invisible.

83% of Africa's workforce — 600 million people — are invisible like her. They have no business records, no data, no credit history. Banks ignore them. Governments can't see them. Markets exploit them.

The Mission

Make the invisible economy visible.

Msaidizi gives every informal worker a voice-powered CFO. Not a loan app. Not a payment tool. A business intelligence system that listens when they speak, tracks their business, and helps them grow.

The Vision

M-Pesa showed cash flow. Msaidizi shows business flow.

When millions of workers track their businesses, something bigger emerges: Africa's Economic Nervous System — real-time economic intelligence from the ground up.

This isn't just an app. It's the infrastructure for the informal economy to become visible, creditworthy, and empowered.
