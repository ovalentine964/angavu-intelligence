Privacy Policy

Your Data Stays on Your Phone

Msaidizi is designed with privacy by architecture:

  • On-device processing: All AI runs on your phone. Your voice, transactions, and business data never leave your device unless you explicitly opt in.
  • No raw data uploaded: We never upload voice recordings, full transcripts, or individual transactions.
  • Anonymized aggregates only: If you opt in, only anonymized, aggregated data is shared — with differential privacy (ε=0.1) and k-anonymity (k≥10).
  • Encrypted storage: Your database is encrypted with SQLCipher (256-bit AES). Post-quantum cryptography protects your data against future threats.
  • You control your data: Delete the app, delete all your data. No cloud backup unless you choose WhatsApp reports.

What We Collect (If You Opt In)

  • Business type and region (for cohort formation)
  • Anonymized transaction summaries (not individual items)
  • Model improvement signals (what the AI got wrong)
  • New vocabulary (product names, dialect terms)

What We Never Collect

  • Voice recordings
  • Full conversation transcripts
  • Individual transaction details
  • Personal identification (name, phone, location)
  • Biometric data
  • PIN or password

Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data at any time.
