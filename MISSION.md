## The Problem

83% of Africa's workforce is informal. They have:
- No data about their economic lives
- No visibility into policies that affect them
- No coordination mechanism to speak with one voice
- No intelligence to see when they're being exploited

Three structural failures keep 600 million people invisible:

### 1. Information Asymmetry
Workers don't know fair prices, competitive interest rates, or what their business is actually worth. Governments pass bills without citizens understanding the cost. Banks charge rates that formal-sector customers would never accept. **Angavu fixes this** — Soko Pulse gives real-time market intelligence, Msaidizi shows daily profit/loss in the worker's own language, and Alama Score tells workers their true financial position.

### 2. Coordination Failures
50 million individuals can't act as one. When Kenya's 2024 Finance Bill was proposed, there was no mechanism for informal workers to understand its impact collectively. People protested because they had no other channel. **Angavu fixes this** — Angavu Pulse aggregates anonymized economic data so policymakers can see what 50 million workers actually need, without requiring those workers to organize.

### 3. Market Exploitation
Informal workers get the worst prices, the worst credit terms, the worst deals — because they have no information and no alternatives. Middlemen rig prices. Lenders charge 15% monthly interest. Suppliers charge 30% more than wholesale. **Angavu fixes this** — by giving every worker a CFO in their pocket that speaks their language and works on their $50 phone.

## The Solution

Angavu Intelligence builds AI-powered economic intelligence for Africa's informal workers. Not charity. Not policy papers. **Infrastructure.**

The same infrastructure that M-Pesa gave to mobile payments — but for economic intelligence.

### Deep Dives
- **[How Angavu Solves Information Asymmetry](docs/SOLUTION_INFORMATION_ASYMMETRY.md)** — Workers don't know fair prices, competitive rates, or their own business health. Angavu collapses the information gap.
- **[How Angavu Solves Coordination Failures](docs/SOLUTION_COORDINATION_FAILURES.md)** — 50 million individuals can't act as one. Angavu provides collective intelligence without collective action.
- **[How Angavu Solves Market Inefficiencies](docs/SOLUTION_MARKET_INEFFICIENCIES.md)** — The informal economy operates at massive inefficiency. Angavu creates price discovery, reduces search costs, and builds missing markets.

### What We Build
- **Msaidizi** — Voice-first AI CFO that speaks 14 African dialects, works offline, costs nothing
- **Soko Pulse** — Real-time market intelligence from informal markets
- **Alama Score** — Financial readiness assessment for the unbanked
- **Angavu Pulse** — MSME activity index for governments and NGOs
- **Jamii Insights** — Financial inclusion metrics for development organizations

### How It Works
Every transaction a worker records through Msaidizi becomes a data point. Federated learning means that data never leaves their phone — but the patterns it reveals power intelligence products that help everyone. More workers → more data → better intelligence → more workers. The flywheel spins.

## The Founder's Story

Valentine Owuor watched his mother — a micro retail trader in Nairobi — work harder than anyone he knows. Count coins at the end of a long day. Figure out profit and loss in her head. Get told she's "just a small trader."

He studied Economics and Statistics at Masinde Muliro University. He learned WHY informal markets fail: information asymmetry, adverse selection, moral hazard, coordination failure. He learned the mathematical models: Bayesian inference, time series, mechanism design.

And he decided: **She deserves better.**

Not charity. Not a handout. **Infrastructure.**
