Privacy Policy — Msaidizi

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

Msaidizi is built by Angavu Intelligence Ltd, based in Migori, Kenya. Our mission is to provide economic intelligence to Africa's informal workers.

Data We Collect

  • Transaction data: Business transactions you record (manually or via M-Pesa SMS capture)
  • Voice data: Voice recordings are processed on-device and are NOT sent to any server
  • Device info: Basic device type and OS version for compatibility
  • Phone number: Used for WhatsApp connection and account verification

How We Use Your Data

  • Generate your business financial reports and insights
  • Provide daily, weekly, and monthly business briefings
  • Improve AI models through federated learning (anonymized, differential privacy)
  • Connect you to financial services when you opt in

Data Storage & Security

  • All financial data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Voice processing happens entirely on your device
  • Data is stored locally on your device first, synced when online
  • We use TLS 1.3 for all network communication

M-Pesa SMS Access

With your permission, Msaidizi reads M-Pesa transaction SMS messages to automatically record your business transactions. This data stays on your device. You can disable this feature at any time in Settings.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. Anonymized, aggregated economic data may be used to generate intelligence reports for businesses and policymakers. Individual workers are never identifiable.

Your Rights

  • View all your data at any time in the app
  • Export your data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Disable M-Pesa SMS capture
  • Opt out of federated learning

Children's Privacy

Msaidizi is not intended for users under 18 years of age.
