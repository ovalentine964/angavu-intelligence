About Angavu Intelligence

We're building the economic nervous system for 2 billion informal workers. From Migori, Kenya to every informal market on earth.

Who We Are

Making the Invisible Economy Visible

Angavu Intelligence is a technology company building AI-powered economic intelligence for the world's largest workforce — informal workers.

Global Mission

Serving 2 billion informal workers across Africa, South Asia, and Southeast Asia. Starting in East Africa.

AI-First

On-device AI superagents, voice-first interfaces, federated learning. Building for environments with no internet.

Privacy by Design

Worker data stays on their device. Post-quantum encryption. Federated learning without sharing individual data.

Founded with Purpose

VO

Valentine Owuor

Founder & CEO

Valentine grew up watching his mother — a retail trader in Nairobi — work harder than anyone he knew, counting coins at the end of long days. She wasn't small. She was invisible. Angavu Intelligence exists to change that for 600 million workers like her.

What Drives Us

Our Values

🌍 Workers First

Every product decision starts with: "Does this help the worker?" Free tools for workers, always. Revenue comes from businesses and governments.

🔒 Privacy by Default

Worker data stays on their device. We never sell individual data. Aggregated intelligence only, with full anonymization.

🎤 Voice First

If you can't explain it by voice, it's not simple enough. Our users speak, not type. Design for their reality.

📊 Data as Infrastructure

We're not building an app. We're building economic infrastructure — the nervous system for the informal economy.
