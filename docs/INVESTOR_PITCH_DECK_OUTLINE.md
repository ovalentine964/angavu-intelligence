## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

**83% of Africa's workforce is invisible.**

- 18.1M informal workers in Kenya alone
- 600M+ across Africa and the Global South
- Zero AI tools built for them
- $465-925M financing gap (DPI Africa, Nov 2025)
- They get the worst prices, worst credit, worst deals

**Why?** Three structural failures:
1. **Information asymmetry** — they don't know fair prices or rates
2. **Coordination failure** — 50M individuals can't act as one
3. **Market inefficiency** — middlemen and lenders exploit the gap
