Diamond-Mortensen-Pissarides (2010 Nobel): markets don't clear because search is expensive. Soko Pulse reduces search to zero. Price indices (Laspeyres, Paasche, Fisher) and ARIMA forecasting for informal markets.

ECO 100 · ECO 204 · STA 245

Privacy First

Your Data Never Leaves Your Phone

We use federated learning to improve our AI without ever seeing your data. Your transactions, your sales, your numbers — they stay on your device.

01
🔐

Federated Learning

Your phone trains a small AI model locally. Only anonymous model improvements — never your actual data — are sent to our server.

02
🛡️

Differential Privacy

Mathematical guarantee (ε=0.1) that no one can reverse-engineer your data from the aggregated model. Used by Apple and Google.

03
👥

K-Anonymity

Your data is only used when at least 5 other workers have similar patterns. You're always hidden in a crowd — impossible to identify individually.

Data Sovereignty
