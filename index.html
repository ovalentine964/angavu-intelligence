FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Is Msaidizi really free?

+

Yes. Msaidizi is free forever for workers. We make money by selling anonymized, aggregated economic intelligence to businesses — not by charging the people who need it most. Your data stays on your phone. You choose what to share.

What phone do I need?

+

Any Android phone with 2GB+ RAM running Android 6.0 or higher. That means Samsung A15, Tecno Spark, Xiaomi Redmi, Nokia G42 — the phones most Kenyans already own. Msaidizi is only 15MB and works completely offline.

Is my data safe?

+

Your raw data never leaves your phone. Voice recordings are processed on-device and deleted. Only anonymized, aggregated data (no names, no phone numbers, no exact locations) is ever shared — and only if you opt in. You can delete everything at any time. We comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

How is this different from M-Shwari or Tala?

+

M-Shwari and Tala lend money. They don't help you run your business. Msaidizi is a business management tool — it tracks your sales, expenses, profits, and debts. It helps you understand your business FIRST, then (when you're ready) connects you to credit based on real business data, not just your M-Pesa history.

Do I need internet to use Msaidizi?

+

No. Msaidizi works 100% offline. The AI runs on your phone. You only need internet if you want to back up your data to the cloud (optional) or download updates. Daily use requires zero data.

Who is behind Biashara Intelligence?

+

Biashara Intelligence was founded by Valentine Owuor, a BSc Economics & Statistics graduate from Migori. The idea came from watching his mother run a business with no tools, no records, and no access to credit. We're based in Migori, Kenya — not Nairobi, not Silicon Valley. We build for the market, from the market.
